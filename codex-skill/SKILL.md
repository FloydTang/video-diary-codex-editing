# Video Diary Codex Edit

Use this skill when the user asks Codex to create or revise a 9:16 personal video diary episode.

## Required Reading

Before editing, read:

- `AGENTS.md`
- `docs/制作标准.md`
- `docs/Codex工作流.md`
- `docs/字幕规范.md`
- `docs/隐私与公开前检查.md`

## Workflow

1. Inspect the episode folder.
2. Secure a usable Chinese SRT.
3. Create polished Chinese and English SRT files.
4. Build a beat sheet around spoken meaning.
5. Make screenshots and recordings evidence-first when they need to be inspected.
6. Compose the video with a full-screen talking-head base, sparse cards, and burned-in bilingual captions.
7. Render a draft.
8. QA safe areas, subtitles, evidence readability, privacy, and output specs.
9. Deliver final paths and caveats.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not overwrite source media.
- Do not summarize the speaker inside the caption layer.
- Do not put important text in the top `0-190px`.
- Do not put captions in the bottom `1620-1920px`.
- Do not make evidence screenshots too small to read.
- Do not leave private information visible.
