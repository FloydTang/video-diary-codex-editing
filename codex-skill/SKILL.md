# Video Diary Codex Edit

Use this skill when the user asks Codex to create or revise a 9:16 personal video diary episode.

## Required Reading

Before editing, read:

- `AGENTS.md`
- `docs/制作标准.md`
- `docs/Codex工作流.md`
- `docs/字幕规范.md`
- `docs/隐私与公开前检查.md`

## Workflow

1. Inspect the episode folder.
2. Secure a usable Chinese SRT.
3. If the raw talking-head footage is unclean, produce a rough-cut mother video, aligned SRT, and delete table, then stop for user confirmation.
4. Create polished Chinese and English SRT files for the confirmed source.
5. Build a beat sheet around spoken meaning.
6. Make screenshots and recordings evidence-first when they need to be inspected.
7. Compose the video with a full-screen talking-head base, sparse cards, and burned-in bilingual captions.
8. Render a draft.
9. QA safe areas, subtitles, evidence readability, privacy, and output specs.
10. Deliver final paths and caveats.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not overwrite source media.
- Do not jump from unclean raw footage into formal packaging. Confirm a rough-cut mother video first.
- Do not hide wrong names or repeated takes by rewriting captions; cut them when possible and flag them when not.
- Do not summarize the speaker inside the caption layer.
- Do not put important text in the top `0-190px`.
- Do not put captions in the bottom `1620-1920px`.
- Do not make evidence screenshots too small to read.
- Do not leave private information visible.
